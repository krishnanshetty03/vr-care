THE CHALLENGE

Why Most Hospital Projects Struggle

Many hospitals face challenges not because of lack of investment… but because of wrong planning.

We commonly see:

Overbuilt infrastructure without matching demand
Wrong specialty mix for the location
Underestimation of operational costs
No clarity on revenue streams
Poor positioning and differentiation
Lack of long-term financial planning
Mistakes made at the planning stage are the most expensive to fix later.
