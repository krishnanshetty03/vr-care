Where Most Projects Go Wrong

Many hospital projects face challenges even before they start operations.

Hospitals built without operational thinking struggle from day one.

Common issues include:

  • Poor planning of departments and flows
  • Infrastructure not aligned with operations
  • Overinvestment in non-critical areas
  • Lack of clarity on staffing and systems
  • No structured pre-launch planning
